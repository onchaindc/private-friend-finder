Guide

How to operate the app

Run a private check

  1. Load sample contacts or upload two CSV files.
  2. Confirm both counters show usable contacts.
  3. Press Find Matches to compute the overlap locally.

Understand the mechanism

Each contact becomes a normalized email or phone key. The app hashes those keys, compares hash sets, and displays only the matches that belong to your local file.

Privacy concept

  • No CSV upload endpoint exists in this demo.
  • Filenames are hidden by default.
  • Solana proof logging never includes raw contacts.
